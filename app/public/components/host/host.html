Become a Host

There are tons of great reasons to offer to host a class at your location

Hosting a class at a business location increases traffic and lets those who otherwise wouldn't know about your business find it! You'll be helping build a stronger community in your city. People will remember the good time they had in the class and positively associate that good feeling with your business. They're are more likely to drive a little further to come back to your location to help you out because you helped them out by hosting the class.

Not only can you host classes taught by others (which is awesome), you can host classes that you are uniquely fit to teach. Pottery shops could have a "How to use a pottery wheel" class. Swing Dancing studio's that already charge for weekly classes could offer a free hour long class every two months to let people learn the basics and try it out for free. If they have a good time, they're bound to sign up for more lessons next week.

Increase traffic to your business while helping those in your community try new things!
